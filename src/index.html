mission

Webを便利にする

The introduction of the Internet brought on big changes to our society.
Today, the speed of communication continues to pick up speed – information is exchanged in a matter of seconds.
No one doubts the usefulness of the Internet, but there are questions about how it is being used. Non-user-friendly applications on an otherwise useful website, unnecessary exchange of information not utilizing on the full potential of a website, all cause unnecessary stress.
Our mission is to discover such useless and non-use-friendly oddities, to find and create solutions to resolve the situation to make useful websites even more user-friendly.

We want to alleviate stress from users, creating for a better future.

私達のミッションはWeb上の無駄で不便な物を発見し、問題解決を行うことで、便利なWebを更に便利にする。そしてそれを使う人々をストレスから開放し、より良い未来を構築することです。
